Sundays

Ingredients

  1. 1 eggs
  2. 3 cooked meat of choice
  3. grated cheese of choice
  4. herbs: parsley, thyme, chives
  5. spices: smoked paprika, cayenne pepper, chilli flakes, cajun spice mix

Steps

Step 1

Preheat the oven to 220? (425?, gas mark 7) Separate the egg white from the yolk Whisk the egg whites in a mixer bowl with a pinch of salt until it forms stiff peaks

Step 2

Gently fold in any chosen ingredients Pile in to nest shapes on a lined baking sheet, one nest per egg used. Bake for 3 minutes, then slip the yolk in to the center of the nest Another 3 minutes in the oven and they should be fluffy, golden mounds, ready to serve
